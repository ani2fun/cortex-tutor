# Step 2 — apply

**Goal:** turn understanding into use — apply the concept to a concrete scenario.

**Gate criterion — `pass` requires:**
- Takes a **specific scenario** (the one posed, or a sensible one of their own) where the concept
  genuinely applies, and
- **reasons through how it applies** — the steps, the outcome, the "so what" — correctly, not just
  naming the concept.

**Pass threshold:** score ≥ 70.

**Retry when:** the answer stays abstract, picks a scenario where the concept doesn't actually apply,
or the reasoning has a correctness error. Point at the gap ("walk me through what happens to the
*second* request"; "that's the definition again — pin it to this scenario").

**Anti-leak:** don't surface the trade-offs or failure modes yet (that's `analyze`). Nudge toward a
concrete example rather than handing one over.
