# Step 4 — defend

**Goal:** commit to a reasoned judgement and stand it up against a counter-point — the payoff of real
understanding.

**Gate criterion — `pass` requires:**
- Makes a **clear judgement or design choice** (use it / don't / use it *here* but not *there*, or
  connects the concept to another idea they know), **justified** with the trade-offs from the previous
  step, and
- **addresses an obvious counter-point** rather than ignoring it.

**Pass threshold:** score ≥ 70.

**Retry when:** the position is unjustified or wishy-washy, ignores the trade-offs already surfaced, or
can't withstand a simple "but what about …?". Offer the counter-point and ask the learner to answer it.

**Anti-leak:** this is the final step. On a genuine `pass`, give a short, warm wrap-up — one thing the
learner understood well and the single key takeaway / how it connects to the bigger picture.
